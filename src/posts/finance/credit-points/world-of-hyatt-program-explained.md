World of Hyatt is the smallest of the major hotel loyalty programs — roughly 1,200 properties compared to Marriott's 8,500. That limited footprint is the most common reason people overlook it. It's also the reason its points are worth significantly more than the competition.

## Why Hyatt Points Are Worth More

Hyatt uses a category-based award chart rather than dynamic pricing for most properties. That chart sets a ceiling on what a hotel can charge in points — regardless of what the cash rate does.

When the Park Hyatt Tokyo costs $800/night in cash, it still costs 35,000 Hyatt points per night. At a conservative valuation of 1.7 cents per point, that's $595 in points value against an $800 cash price.

## Understanding the Category System

World of Hyatt divides its properties into eight categories, with award rates scaling from Category 1 to Category 8.

| Category | Standard Points/Night | Peak Points/Night | Off-Peak Points/Night |
|---|---|---|---|
| Category 1 | 3,500 | 5,000 | 2,500 |
| Category 2 | 6,500 | 8,500 | 4,500 |
| Category 3 | 9,000 | 12,000 | 6,500 |
| Category 4 | 12,000 | 16,000 | 8,500 |
| Category 5 | 17,000 | 21,000 | 13,000 |
| Category 6 | 22,000 | 27,000 | 17,000 |
| Category 7 | 30,000 | 37,000 | 23,000 |
| Category 8 | 40,000 | 50,000 | 30,000 |

**The fifth-night-free benefit:** A stay of five or more consecutive nights at the same property costs the equivalent of four nights in points — a 20% discount. Always book multi-night stays as a single reservation.

## Best Ways to Earn Hyatt Points

**Chase Ultimate Rewards (1:1 transfer)** — The single most powerful Hyatt earning strategy. Transfer <a href="/posts/finance/credit-points/chase-ultimate-rewards-maximum-value/">Chase Ultimate Rewards</a> points to World of Hyatt at a 1:1 ratio instantly. More on this below.

**On-property stays (5x base)** — 5 points per $1 spent on qualifying charges. Always attach your loyalty number before checking in.

**World of Hyatt Credit Card (4x on Hyatt stays)** — 4x on Hyatt stays, 2x on dining, airlines, and transit. $95 annual fee with one free night certificate annually.

**Hyatt Dining Club (2x)** — Earn points at participating restaurants worldwide by registering your credit card.

## Best Redemptions in Every Category

**Category 3-4 (9,000-12,000 pts/night) — The sweet spot.** This is where Hyatt's category system delivers its best consistent value. Category 3-4 includes many full-service Hyatt Regency properties in major cities and excellent Andaz properties in secondary markets. The Park Hyatt Buenos Aires (Category 4) goes for $300+ in cash — a remarkable value at 12,000 points.

**Category 5-6 (17,000-22,000 pts/night) — Flagship resort properties.** The <a href="/posts/travel/hotel-reviews/andaz-maui-review/">Andaz Maui at Wailea</a> sits at Category 6 (22,000 pts/night standard). Cash rates run $700-1,000/night. At 22,000 points, you're consistently getting 3.5-4.5 cents per point — more than double the minimum target.

**Category 7-8 (30,000-40,000 pts/night) — Luxury flagships.** Park Hyatt Tokyo (30,000 pts) against $600+/night cash. Alila Ventana Big Sur (30,000 pts) against $1,000+/night. The fifth-night-free benefit is especially powerful here.

## Elite Status — Is It Worth Chasing?

**Discoverist (10 nights):** Complimentary room upgrades when available, late checkout at 2pm. Achievable by anyone who stays at Hyatt a handful of times per year. The upgrade benefit alone often pays for multiple nights of effort. See our guide on <a href="/posts/travel/hotel-reviews/how-to-negotiate-hotel-upgrade/">how to negotiate a hotel upgrade</a> to maximize this even at base tier.

**Explorist (30 nights):** Confirmed room upgrades at booking including standard suites, Club lounge access, 20% bonus points.

**Globalist (60 nights):** Confirmed suite upgrades, complimentary breakfast at most properties, guaranteed 4pm late checkout. The breakfast benefit alone saves $30-60 per person per day — meaningful over a 5-night couples trip.

## Mistakes That Waste Your Points

**Redeeming for cash or statement credits** at 1-1.2 cents per point — significantly below the 1.7+ cents achievable through hotel redemptions.

**Booking Category 1-2 properties when cash rates are low.** If the hotel costs $80 cash and 6,500 points, you're getting 1.2 cents per point. Pay cash and save points for better value.

**Transferring speculatively from Chase.** UR points can become Hyatt points instantly — the reverse is impossible. Only transfer when you have a confirmed booking.

**Missing the fifth-night-free benefit.** Book multi-night stays as a single reservation, not split bookings.

**Letting points expire.** Hyatt points expire after 24 months of account inactivity. A single qualifying transaction resets the clock.
